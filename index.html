1. How do I get started?

Assuming you got to this page the regular way, you have already completed onboarding and are already set to start using the app! However before we proceed, you can visit the Settings page from the top navigation bar to change any settings you set if you changed your mind. You can now click the "" button in the bottom right corner, or the "Add" button in the navigation bar to open the Add a New Body Part prompt. From there, it is as simple as clicking on the body parts you would like to work on or improve, and then click Submit! The program will do the rest of the work, creating a personalized plan to target the muscles you selected. You will be able to see some days on the calendar have been populated with a "" icon: clicking days with this icon will show you what needs to be done on that particular day. Clicking on an exercise in the information bar on the left will bring you to the exercise encyclopedia where you can find more information.

2. How can I find more information about an exercise?

Clicking the Exercises button in the navigation bar will lead you to a dialog which allows you to select the name of any exercise, and the app will give you all the information you may need: such as instructions, images, and if any equipment is required.

3. How can I change the accent colour of the app?

Click the Settings button in the navigation bar to open the Settings dialog. From there, you will be able to select a new accent colour for the application. If the colour you selected is invalid, the preview window will turn translucent and will not save until you select a valid colour.

4. Can I import or export the data from the app?

Yes! To do this, click the Settings button in the navigation bar to open the Settings dialog. From there, you will be able to click the Import or Export buttons to perform the actions. The settings will be exported as a json file.

5. Where is my data stored?

All the data for the app is stored locally on-device. Clearing site data for this website will clear your settings, and any routines you may have saved, so be careful when clearing your cookies. If you are worried about losing your data, make sure to do regular backups by exporting your data.

6. I want to create a routine for a day other than today. How would I do this?

At this current moment in time, it is not possible to create a routine which starts on any day other than the current date.
